Mason Protter (Nov 16 2021 at 20:01):

You could use an inner constructor to reject invalid data

julia> struct 𝔹²
           x::Int
           y::Int
           𝔹²(x,y) = if (x,y) ∈ Iterators.product((0,1), (0,1))
               new(x,y)
           else
               throw("Invalid booleans $((x,y))")
           end
       end
