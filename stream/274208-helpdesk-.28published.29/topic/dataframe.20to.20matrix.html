Mason Protter (Mar 31 2022 at 22:42):

The fastest method I've fouind so far is

let arr = Matrix{Float64}(undef, length(unique($df.x)), length(unique($df.y)))
    gdf = groupby($df, :x)
    for (i, sdf) ∈ enumerate(gdf)
        arr[i, :] .= sdf.z
    end
    arr
end

but it's ugly and not very generic
