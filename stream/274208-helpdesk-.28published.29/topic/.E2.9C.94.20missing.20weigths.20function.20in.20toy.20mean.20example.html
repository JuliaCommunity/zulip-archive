Leandro Martínez (Jul 28 2023 at 18:40):

This is an example of the StatsBase package, not of Base.Statistics:

julia> using StatsBase

julia> a = [1,2,3];

julia> w = [3,4,5];

julia> mean(a, weights(w))
2.1666666666666665
